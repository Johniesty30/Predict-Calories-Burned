About Us

We are third-semester students from the Informatics Engineering program at Universitas Negeri Semarang (UNNES). As part of our final project for the Discrete Mathematics course, we are developing a project focused on the application of machine learning, specifically using the Random Forest algorithm.

Our project aims to predict the number of calories burned by gym members based on a dataset sourced from Kaggle. By leveraging machine learning techniques, we hope to provide insights that help individuals better understand their calorie-burning patterns based on the physical activities they engage in.

This project is not only an academic requirement but also our initial step in exploring the broader applications of computer science in real-world contexts, particularly in the fields of health and fitness. We believe that the synergy between technology and data can drive innovations that benefit society. Thank you for choosing our website.
